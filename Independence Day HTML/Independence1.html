Independence Day

Independence day is celebrated on 15 August every year. This festival is celebrated because on 15th August 1947 India was an Independent nation and was no more in the control of britishers.

On This day Indians gained freedom from britishers which were ruling on india from the long time. And due to help of our freedom fighters, many politicans, etc. We gained independence.

Every year on this day from now we hosts Indian flags and celebrate our freedom and independence and feel proud to be Indian.
