How do we celebrate Indepedence Day

Independence Day is celebrated all over India cheerfully and In Redfort Flag Hosting is done not only on redfort but even in offices , school, collages,etc. and then at India Gate we see parade passing through India Gate and Parade is so cheerful that the people who see are proud to be Indians and feel how powerful is their nation. Then Our Prime Minister Salute to the army , navy, air force and the to all the freedom fighters who scarifised themselfves for Independence of India.

At the time of flag hosting people stand in attention for the respect of India After Flag Hosting at schools, collages and offices, sweets are distributed.

At India Gate there is a huge crowd who gathers there and celebrate independence day and proud to be Indian.
